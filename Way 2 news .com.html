World Bamboo Day – History

The 8th meeting of the World Bamboo Organization (WBO) was held in Bangkok in 2009. Representatives from 100 countries attended. In recognition of the importance of bamboo, it was proposed at this meeting to celebrate World Bamboo Day on September 18 every year.. All the representatives approved it. Since then, World Bamboo Day has been celebrated on SEP-18 every year.. The importance of bamboo, the benefits it brings to the environment and the economy are being explained to the public.
